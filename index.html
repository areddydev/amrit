About Me

Thank you for visiting my webpage.

I am an Assistant Professor in the Computer Science department (joint appointment with the ECE department) at the University of Central Florida, USA. Before joining UCF, I was an Assistant Research Professor/Scientist at the University of Maryland (UMD), College Park, MD, USA, working closely with Prof. Dinesh Manocha, Prof. Pratap Tokekar, and Prof. Furong Huang. Prior to my time at UMD, I was fortunate enough to work with Dr. Alec Koppel and Dr. Brian Sadler at the US Army Research Laboratory, where I gained valuable experience and insights into the real-world applications of my research. I earned my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) in Kanpur under the supervision of Prof. Ketan Rajawat, where I focused on distributed and online learning with stochastic gradient methods for my Ph.D. thesis.

Research Interests

My research work is dedicated to advancing the forefront of machine learning (ML) and artificial intelligence (AI), focusing on critical areas that shape the future of technology and society. As we navigate the complexities of AI, my research endeavors to harness its potential responsibly and innovatively. My core areas of interest are

  • AI Alignment and Ethical Integration
  • Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback
  • Safety/Trustworthiness in Generative AI
